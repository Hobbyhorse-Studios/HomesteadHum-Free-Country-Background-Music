# HomesteadHum-Free-Country-Background-Music
Generative country &amp; folk background music, made in your browser. Real tunes, no samples, no vocals, no copyright.

Check it out on itch!
https://hobbyhorsestudios.itch.io/homesteadhum


Homestead Hum is a generative music console that plays endless country and folk ambiance right in your browser. Pick a mood, hit Play, and it composes a real tune in real time — proper repeating melodies and phrases, not random noise — then lets you download as much of it as you want as a clean WAV. Every sound is synthesized live from scratch, so there are no samples and no copyright. Whatever you make is yours, free, forever.

Built for creators who need warm background music on tap: YouTube and streaming backdrops, homestead and country vlogs, game prototypes, podcasts, tabletop sessions, or anywhere you want that back-porch feel without licensing headaches. No vocals, so it never fights your narration.

Ten moods Lazy Sunday · Front-Porch Evening · Lonesome Prairie · Folk · Hoedown · Gospel · Campfire · Morning Chores · Bluegrass Ramble · Dust Bowl

Build your own band Toggle seven instruments on or off and set each one's volume: banjo, fiddle, acoustic guitar, upright bass, mandolin, harmonica, and bells. Whichever instruments you turn on share the melody and back each other up.

Tweak it your way Key, tempo, room/reverb, and busyness controls, plus a full Mix & Tone stage — bass, mid, treble, warmth, width, and master. Hit New Tune any time for a fresh melody.

Download Render any track from 1 to 30 minutes and download it as a WAV. No watermarks, no attribution required.

The fine print (or lack of it) No samples. No copyright. No account, no install — one page that runs offline once loaded. Everything it makes is royalty-free and yours.
